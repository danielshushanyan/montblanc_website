ACHIEVING PERFECTION

Pure grain alcohol undergoes a 5-step distillation process under the supervision of experienced artisans.

Through their knowledge of the secrets of the art of distillation, French craftsmen produce the highest quality product and achieve the optimal flavor.

The high degree of purification means the product has a uniquely smooth and harmonious flavor and neutral aroma, which is as smooth as the snow on the Alps. The note of alpine freshness bestows the drink the desired pure taste and aroma.

Alan Vincent – the master blender!

Alan boasts the honorary title of cellar master in the winemaking industry in France.

Alan Vincent is responsible for every step in the production of MONT BLANC vodka and for ensuring its exemplary quality in accordance with all the traditions of this region and the wisdom of winemakers of old.
