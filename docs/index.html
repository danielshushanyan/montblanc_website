The heartland of a masterpiece

Mont Blanc vodka is produced in the France, known for its fertile soils and pure artesian water from the central mountain range, as well as its fantastic crop growing traditions and fine food, wines and spirits. No wonder this is the best place to produce a masterpiece!

HISTORY OF HERITAGE

The finest wheat in the north of France

The production of Mont Blanc vodka begins with 100% high-quality French wheat. We use the best variety grown in the north of France, the homeland of Picardian wheat. This wheat gives high-quality French bread its richness and flavor, and is without doubt the best in Europe.

PURE PERFECTION

Spring water for a smooth and velvety flavor

Crystal clear spring water from the Angeac-Champagne irrigates the soil of the France region from the Central Mountain Range and bestows the vodka a diamond-like clarity. The Vodka acquires a smooth, well-rounded and slightly velvety flavor. It is so pure and smooth that it requires very little extra filtration.

5 times distilled vodka

Notes of Alpine freshness

Pure grain alcohol undergoes a 5-step distillation process under the supervision of experienced distillers. Through their knowledge of the secrets of the art of distillation, French artisans produce the highest quality product and achieve the optimal flavor. The high degree of purification means the product has a uniquely smooth and harmonious flavor and neutral aroma, which is as smooth as the snow on the Alps. The note of alpine freshness bestows the drink the desired pure taste and aroma.

One of the important components in MONT BLANC vodka is the use of pure spring water from a natural source in Angeac-Champagne. The purest water from an underground source bestows the vodka a diamond-like clarity. The vodka acquires a smooth, well-rounded and slightly velvety flavor.

THE HEIGHT OF PERFECTION

MONT BLANC is a mountain in France which is part of a snowy mountain range whose highest summit reaches 4,810 meters. The highest point of the Alps and Europe, it embodies tranquility, beauty, crystal purity and balance. Mont Blanc Vodka is a brand that can ascend to the top in any business and which knowns no boundaries.
